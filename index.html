📖 How to Use ImperialPredict

1. The Basics

Select your course and enter your marks (0-100) using the inputs or sliders. Your data auto-saves to your browser, so you'll never lose your progress.

2. Checkboxes & "Ignored" Work

Uncheck any assignments or whole modules you haven't completed yet. The calculator will completely ignore them and re-weight your year average based only on the work you've actually done.

3. 🛡️ Pass Mode (40%)

Toggle Pass Mode at the top to temporarily treat all your unchecked assignments as a bare pass (40%). This reveals your absolute "worst-case scenario" floor grade if you just scrape by on the rest of the year.

4. 🎯 Target Predictor & Auto-Fill

Select a Target Degree (e.g., 2:1) from the dropdown. The app will calculate the exact average you need on your remaining (unchecked) work.

  • Click 🪄 Auto-Fill Target Grades to instantly snap all unchecked sliders to the exact marks required.
  • Once auto-filled, drag any purple slider up or down. The other purple sliders will automatically adjust to balance out your target average!
  • If you are playing with the purple sliders and decide "I can definitely get an 80% on this specific coursework," just check its box. This locks that grade in as an achieved score, preventing the See-Saw from altering it further.

5. Module Insights & Trajectory

Click the ℹ️ Insights button on a module to view its historical difficulty and read specific examiner feedback on where past students lost marks. The Trajectory Graph at the top of the page tracks your genuine performance across the academic terms.

Privacy Note: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not see your grades. Hit the 🗑️ Reset Grades button to clear your data completely.
